Who We Are

THE VIBE

Fried Vibez is Rockdale's go-to spot for Nashville hot chicken done right. We're talking crispy, juicy, fire-level heat with flavours that hit different.

Started by your one and only horse guy 🐎, we set out to bring the real Nashville experience to Sydney — bold sauces, loaded fries, smash burgers and tenders that keep you coming back every time.

We're open 7 days a week until 10:30pm, so whether it's a late-night craving or a family feed, the vibez are always right.
